Facilities at PVPSIT

Facilities

Our college provides top-notch facilities to ensure the best experience for our students. These include:

  • Library: A fully equipped library with digital and physical resources for academic excellence.
  • Laboratories: State-of-the-art labs for practical learning and research across all disciplines.
  • Hostel: Comfortable and secure on-campus accommodations with all necessary amenities.
  • Cafeteria: A hygienic and affordable cafeteria offering a variety of food options.
  • Sports Facilities: Extensive facilities for indoor and outdoor games, including cricket, basketball, and more.
  • Medical Center: A health center with trained medical professionals available 24/7.
  • Wi-Fi: Campus-wide high-speed internet connectivity for academic and personal use.
