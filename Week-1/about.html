About Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology

Brief Profile

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT), established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. The college is sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, the premier organization that runs 18 educational institutions in diverse fields of study. The college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

The institution is autonomous, approved by AICTE, permanently affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada, and accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. The institution is ISO 9001-2015 certified and widely acclaimed for its quality standards. UGC has accorded 2f / 12B status, and the government of Andhra Pradesh has conferred an A grade to the institution.

The college is placed in the band of 101 to 150 in the NIRF Innovation stream.

Vision

To provide a rich ambience for academic and professional excellence, research, employability skills, entrepreneurship, and social responsibility.

Mission

To empower the students with technical knowledge, awareness of up-to-date technical trends, inclination for research in areas of human needs, capacity building for employment/entrepreneurship, and application of technology for societal needs.

Hallmarks

  • ISO 9001-2015 certified institution.
  • NAAC A+ grade accreditation.
  • All UG programs accredited by NBA.
  • 100% ragging-free environment.
  • Autonomous status since 2012.
  • Strong training and placement cell.
  • Entrepreneurship support and industry partnerships.
  • Active NSS and NCC wings promoting social responsibility.
  • Technical/professional societies for learning beyond the classroom.

IQAC (Internal Quality Assurance Cell)

The primary aim of the IQAC is to develop a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution. It facilitates the creation of a learner-centric environment, ensures the quality of academic programs, and coordinates various quality-related activities within the institution.

Objectives of IQAC

  • Ensuring timely, efficient, and progressive performance of academic, administrative, and financial tasks.
  • Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment conducive to quality education.
  • Optimizing the integration of modern methods of teaching and learning.
  • Ensuring the credibility of evaluation procedures and enhancing institutional performance.
  • Supporting research, innovation, and faculty development programs.
