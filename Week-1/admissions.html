Admission Procedure - PVPSIT

Admission Procedure

Admission into UG and PG programmes of the Institution is based on State level Common entrance tests conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Undergraduate Engineering Programme: Admission is based on State Level Common Entrance Test i.e., EAMCET / ECET, conducted by Government of Andhra Pradesh.

MBA Course: Admission is based on the state level common entrance test, ICET, conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

M.Tech Programme: Admission is based on PGECET conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh and all India GATE exam.

Eligibility

The eligibility criteria for admission into UG & PG programmes are as per the norms approved by Government of Andhra Pradesh from time to time.

Categories

Category - A:

For both U.G and P.G Programs, 70% of the seats will be allocated based on the common entrance test i.e., EAMCET/ICET/PGECET conducted by Government of Andhra Pradesh and GATE Rank based for M.Tech Admission.

Category - B:

30% of the seats are filled under Management / NRI category as per the norms / regulations prescribed by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education.

Lateral Entry Programmes: 10% of the Actual Intake and Vacancy Seats of Convener Quota in Previous Year (If any) seats are filled under the Lateral Entry based on the common entrance test i.e., ECET conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
