About Us

The Training and Placement Cell of PVP Siddhartha is established with the sole aim of finding suitable employment opportunities for all eligible students. It caters to all the training requirements of students through highly experienced faculty.

The unique program of skill enhancement identifies and nurtures the talent in job aspirants and ignites the young minds to capably and confidently face the recruitment drives.

The placement cell has state-of-the-art infrastructure and a rich ambiance to facilitate the training process. The students of PVP are provided excellent training that enhances their potential and improves their confidence levels.

The facilities include a well-furnished Auditorium, GD rooms, closed interview panels, and conference halls.

Activities

  • Mock Interviews: A practice job interview simulating the actual interview process.
  • Group Discussions: A comparative group activity for candidate selection.
  • Public Speaking: Communication of information to an audience.
  • Personality Development: Enhancing and grooming one's skills and self-confidence.
  • Career Guidance: Services to help manage career development.
  • Aptitude and Reasoning Sessions: Tests to assess logical reasoning and thinking performance.
  • Campus Recruitment Drives: Programs conducted to provide jobs to students.
  • Motivational Lectures: Lectures to motivate and inspire students.
  • Corporate Grooming: Development of professional skills for corporate environments.
